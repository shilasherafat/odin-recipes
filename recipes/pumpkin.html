Paul's Pumpkin Bars

Description

These easy pumpkin bars are full of fall flavor with a moist texture and a decadent cream cheese frosting. So far, I haven't found anyone who doesn't love them!

Ingredients

For the cake:

  • Eggs
  • Pumpkin puree
  • White sugar
  • Vegetable oil
  • All-purpose flour
  • Cinnamon
  • Baking soda
  • Baking powder
  • Salt

For the frosting:

  • Butter
  • Cream cheese
  • Vanilla extract
  • Confectioners' sugar

Steps

  1. Beat the wet ingredients in a bowl until well-combined.
  2. Sift the dry ingredients together in a separate bowl, then add the dry mixture to the wet mixture.
  3. Spread the batter in an ungreased jelly roll pan.
  4. Bake in a preheated oven until the cake bounces back when gently pressed.
  5. Beat the butter and cream cheese together until smooth, then stir in the vanilla.
  6. Add the confectioners' sugar a little at a time, beating until mixture is smooth.
  7. Evenly spread the frosting over the cooled cake.
  8. Cut into 24 equal squares.
